How to play

It's simple!

Plug in your mouse, keep your cursor inside the window while making sure to avoid any obstacles that may appear in front of you.

If you get hit, just move your cursor out of the window for a quick second for a seamless restart.

!Before playing, make sure to prepare yourself for unexpected frustrations, bugs, seizures or laughter!
